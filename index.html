About D3

D3 is an interdisciplinary conference & expo focused on developing creative talent in the areas of Commercial Arts, Motion Design and Visual Effects. D3 brings together a diverse community of independent thinkers who work on projects rooted in these emergent creative industries. This conference is more than design tips; it’s about developing and maintaining a collaborative design community in south Florida. Hosted by Miami Dade College’s School of Entertainment & Design Technology in collaboration with The Idea Center.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire, spark innovation and promote collaborative, sustainable creative relationships. Enjoy 4 days of events designed to connect young, aspiring artists and designers with experienced professionals in their industries, and foster empowering relationships. Young creative talent can show off their portfolios, find mentors, receive advice, refine their skills or learn new ones in specialized workshops.

Event FAQs

Do I have to bring my printed ticket to the event?

Please bring your printed ticket to the event, or have your ticket open on your smart phone.

What are my parking options at the event?

Monday, April 4: Free Parking!

An MDC parking garage is available on NE 5th ST between NE 1st AVE and NE 2nd AVE. - Building 7 Parking Garage

There are several other public/private lots and garages within walking distance.


Tuesday April 5 - Thursday, April 7: Free Parking!

(North Campus student parking)

Where is the conference located?

This conference has 2 locations, as follows:
Monday, April 4: The Idea Center at Miami Dade College Wolfson Campus

Tuesday-Friday, April 5 - 8: Miami Dade College, North Campus

Can I get hired for a job while at the expo?

There will be a chance for you to meet with job recruiters and potentially schedule job interviews.

Can I speak with more than one mentor?

If you would like to speak with multiple mentors you must register ahead of time for the according number of time slots you would like within the ones available.

How long are the mentor sessions?

Mentor sessions will be about 10 minutes long.

Will lunch be included at the event?

Lunch will be included, you are also allowed to bring your own lunch or enjoy one of a variety of food options nearby.

Will Wifi be available at the Expo?

Wifi will be available for your convenience.

What should I bring to the event?

We recommend bringing a laptop/iPad, notebook, pen/pencil, and any other note taking materials as well as your portfolio and/or resume for the chance to have your work seen by professionals in the industry and hiring recruiters; and of course, remember to bring your appetite for exciting new knowledge and opportunities!

Do I have to pay for parking?

FREE parking will be available for all attendees Monday April 4 - Thursday April 7.
On Friday April 8th, metered street parking will be available.

How early can I arrive to the Expo?

Arriving 15-30 minutes prior to the expo start time should give you enough time to find parking and walk to the event. Doors open at 9am.

Is there a dress code?

We suggest dressing business casual-trendy. Let your individual designer character shine and be ready to speak with professionals in the industry that could potentially set up future job interviews for you while at the Expo.

Do I have to pay to attend the Expo?

The event is, and will always be, FREE for college students who register by the given deadline; and for a limited time only we are allowing all guests to attend free of charge. For future events non-student guests will be charged a fee for workshop events. Feel free to invite family or friends to this event!
