"Dream Big" Poster Design Contest

  • Submission Deadline: April 1, 2016

About the Competition:

The competition will take place during Miami Dade College’s D3 Conference and Expo 2016. Details TBA soon! Design a poster with a creative concept that can tell a story about "dreaming big". Designers should think outside of the box and create innovative ways of representing their interpretations of the “Dream Big Theme”.


Prizes:

One (1) winner will be chosen by our panel of design judges to receive the grand prize (TBA).

At the time of voting, employers will also be on the lookout to hire creative freelancers and very talented designers as long term employees.


Rules:

  • One (1) entry per person

  • There will be (1) winner

  • Open to current college students and recent graduates (up to two (2) years after graduation)


Submission Guidelines:

  • File size must be 18in x 24in @ 300 dpi

  • File format must be PDF document format

  • Upload your final PDF design to your own online storage account (ex: dropbox or google drive)

  • Share the link to your work to: poster2016@d3expo.com before the Submission Deadline (April 1, 2016 at 12:00pm)
