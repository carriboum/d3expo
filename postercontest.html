"Dream Big" Poster Design Contest

  • Submission Deadline: April 18, 2016

About the Competition:

This years conference theme is “Dream Big”. “The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams.” The most creative submissions that reflect a visual interpretation of this quote from Eleanor Roosevelt will have the best shot of winning the contest. The competition will start taking submissions on April 4th, during Miami Dade College’s D3 216 Conference & Expo 2016.


Prizes:

1st, 2nd and 3rd place winners will be chosen by our panel of design professionals and get a public announcement on our website and all of our social media platforms. The winning contestant will win one our amazing prizes from one of our sponsors and the poster will serve as the official poster for D3 2016 (artist will be fully credited for artwork).


Deadlines:

Open for submissions on April 4th (open to all ages and skill levels)

Submission Deadline is Monday, April 18 / Winners will be announced on Friday, April 22

Rules:

  • One (1) entry per person

  • There will be (1) winner

  • Open to current college students and recent graduates (up to two (2) years after graduation)


Submission Guidelines:

  • Share the link to your artwork to: contest@d3expo.com

  • Submission Deadline is April 18, 2016 at 12:00pm
